## **<u>I successfully withdrew ATTN - what’s next?</u>**

**Check Your Balance**\
Make sure the ATTN amount is visible in your Collective Memory account. On the CM app, go to:\
**Wallet \> ATTN – Attention Token** (under **TOKENS**).

**Decide What to Do Next**\
You can either:

- **Keep the ATTN token** in your wallet, or
- **Swap it to USDC** if you want to sell it for cash.

**Learn More**\
Before deciding, we recommend reading about the ATTN token and its potential on our relevant [blog post](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/guides/attention-as-currency).

## **<u>How to Swap Your ATTN to USDC?</u>**

There are a few ways to swap ATTN to USDC within the Collective Memory (CM) app:

### **Option 1: Swap and Send Using the CM App**

1. Go to Wallet \> SWAP \> Swap ATTN to USDC.
2. After swapping, you can send the USDC to an external wallet or another swapping app:\
   Wallet \> TRANSFER \> Send.

**⚠️ Important: USDC transfers from the Collective Memory app are sent via the Base network. Please ensure the receiving wallet supports Base before transferring.**

### **Option 2: Use an External Wallet App**

1. Export your CM wallet to an external wallet (e.g., Base, Uniswap):\
   Wallet \> TRANSFER \> Settings \> Account & Security \> Recovery Phrase. **Please note:** You will receive an email titled “Your wallet private key was exported” at the email address associated with your account. This is a standard security notification, and since you initiated the action, there is no cause for concern. If you receive this email without requesting to view or export your keys, please contact the support team immediately.
2. Using the unique Recovery Phrase \> Import your wallet into the external app and perform swaps there.

**⚠️ Important: Never share your unique Recovery Phrase with anyone!**

## UPDATE — June 25, 2026

As of June 25, 2026, we have increased the security level of our wallets. As a result, Recovery Phrases are no longer available for new wallets created on or after this date.

### What Are Your Options?

- Use the **Swap** and **Send** options directly in the Collective Memory app.
- If available in your region, you can also use the **Debit Card** option. For more information, please contact our support team.
- You may create a new wallet using the Base or Uniswap apps and transfer your funds from your Collective Memory wallet to the new wallet, to perform all swaps from these apps.\
  **Please use only these apps, as they support both USDC on Base and ATTN on Base.**

## Important Notes & Safety Tips

- Never share your Recovery Phrase with anyone.
- If this is your first transfer, we recommend sending a small test amount (approximately \$5–10 USD) first to make sure everything works correctly.
- Do not send ATTN directly to external wallets or exchanges (such as Binance or Bybit). Always swap ATTN to USDC first.\
  You can do this in the Collective Memory app: **Wallet → Swap**.
- Transfers to and from the Collective Memory app are processed on the Base network. Before making a transfer, please make sure the receiving wallet or exchange supports the Base network.

For additional help at any stage, please contact: [support@collectivememory.ai](mailto:support@collectivememory.ai)

## Selling USDC for Cash

Once you have swapped your funds to USDC, you can sell or trade it using external services such as Binance P2P, Transak, Bybit, and others.
