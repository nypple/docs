**<u>Where do I direct technical questions regarding my account?</u>**

If you have any technical questions or issues related to your Collective Memory account, please contact our support team at [**support@collectivememory.ai**](mailto:support@collectivememory.ai).

To help us assist you as quickly as possible, please include the following information in your email:

- Your **full name** and **country**
- Your **account ID** or **wallet address**
- A **screenshot of the issue/error** as it appears on your end

If additional details are needed, our team will request them through email correspondence.
