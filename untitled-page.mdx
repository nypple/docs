# 

## How do Investor Benefits Packages work?

Investor Benefits Packages provide enhanced staking capacity, increased withdrawal limits (for subscription plans), and pATTN rewards. Benefits are activated immediately after purchase or qualifying conversion and remain active according to the package selected.

---

## What is the difference between Subscription and One-Off packages?

### Subscription Packages

Subscription packages provide:

- Increased staking capacity
- Enhanced withdrawal limits
- Monthly pATTN rewards
- Monthly bonus pATTN rewards

Benefits remain active while the subscription is maintained.

### One-Off Packages

One-off packages provide:

- Increased staking capacity
- pATTN rewards equivalent to the package value

These packages are purchased once and do not automatically renew.

---

## Do I need a credit card to access Investor Benefits?

No.

For users who do not have access to a credit card, Investor Benefits can also be activated through a qualifying **ATTN** or **USDC conversion**.

This option was specifically created to ensure all users can access Investor Benefits regardless of their preferred payment method or geographic location.

---

## Can I activate Investor Benefits using ATTN?

Yes.

You can activate Investor Benefits for **30 days** by converting the equivalent package value in **ATTN**.

For example:

| Investor Tier | Equivalent Value |
| :-- | :-- |
| Plus Investor | \$195 worth of ATTN |
| Advanced Investor | \$445 worth of ATTN |
| Pro Investor | \$945 worth of ATTN |

The required ATTN amount is calculated using the live ATTN/USD exchange rate at the time of activation.\
\
Learn how to purchase and convert ATTN to pATTN here - [https://docs.collectivememory.ai/purchase-attn-and-p-attn#purchase-attn-and-pattn](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/purchase-attn-and-p-attn#purchase-attn-and-pattn)

Once the conversion is completed, your selected Investor Benefits tier will be activated for the next 30 days.

---

## Can I activate Investor Benefits using USDC?

Yes.

You can activate Investor Benefits for **30 days** by converting the equivalent value in **USDC**.

For example:

| Investor Tier | USDC Equivalent |
| :-- | :-- |
| Plus Investor | 195 USDC |
| Advanced Investor | 445 USDC |
| Pro Investor | 945 USDC |

Learn how to purchase and convert USDC to ATTN, and ATTN to pATTN here - [https://docs.collectivememory.ai/purchase-attn-and-p-attn#purchase-attn-and-pattn](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/purchase-attn-and-p-attn#purchase-attn-and-pattn)\
\
Once the conversion is completed, the corresponding Investor Benefits tier will be activated for the next 30 days.

---

## How long do benefits remain active when activated through an ATTN or USDC conversion?

Benefits remain active for **30 days** from the date of activation.

At the end of the 30-day period, users may renew their benefits through another qualifying ATTN or USDC conversion or by maintaining an active subscription.

---

## Is there any difference between subscription activation and ATTN/USDC activation?

No.

Users receive the same Investor Benefits regardless of whether they activate them through:

- An active subscription
- A qualifying ATTN conversion
- A qualifying USDC conversion

The benefit tier, staking limits, withdrawal allowances, and reward structure remain the same for the duration of the activation period.

---

## How is the ATTN conversion amount calculated?

The required ATTN amount is determined using the live ATTN/USD market rate at the time of activation.

Because market prices fluctuate, the amount of ATTN required to activate a package may vary over time.

---

## What is pATTN?

pATTN is a platform reward token distributed as part of Investor Benefits packages.

Eligible users receive pATTN rewards based on the package or benefit tier they have activated.

---

## What is Bonus pATTN?

Bonus pATTN is an additional promotional reward provided with subscription-based Investor Benefits tiers.

Bonus pATTN is credited separately from standard rewards and is not redeemable.

---

## How is pATTN converted to ATTN?

The current conversion ratio is:

**4 pATTN = 1 ATTN**

This ratio may be adjusted in accordance with future platform updates.

---

## Are package benefits guaranteed to remain unchanged?

Package offerings, rewards, and benefit structures may evolve over time.

Any changes will be communicated through official platform announcements and documentation updates.

---

## Can I upgrade my Investor Benefits tier?

Yes.

Users may upgrade to a higher Investor Benefits tier at any time. Any additional requirements or activation costs will be displayed during the upgrade process.

---

## Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes.

Subscription-based Investor Benefits can be canceled at any time. Benefits will remain active until the end of the current billing period and will not automatically renew afterward.
