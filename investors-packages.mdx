# How to Purchase

## Purchase with a Credit Card

1. Update the app to the latest version (released on Monday, June 1st, at 6:00 PM CET).
2. Open the app and navigate to the **User Panel**.
3. Click **Capital In**.
4. You will be redirected to your web account management portal and automatically authenticated.
5. Follow the on-screen instructions, select your package, and complete the purchase using a credit card.

## Don't Have a Credit Card?

You can still access package benefits by using crypto:

1. Convert your USDC or ATTN into one of the eligible package amounts. 
2. Once the conversion is complete, the corresponding package benefits will be applied automatically.
3. Your active package status will appear under **Capital In** in the User Panel.
4. We'll consider this purchase as the first installment of a subscription plan. To keep your subscription active using cryptocurrency payments, please make another payment within 30 days. We call this an **Optimistic Subscription**, and we created it to support customers who do not have convenient access to international credit cards.

> **Note:** If you do not see the purchase option, make sure you are running the latest version of the app and restart it after updating.
