## **Why do I see changes in my ranking and withdrawable amount?**

Your Mapper ranking is based on your overall contribution across three key activities:

- **Content creation**
- **Staking on content**
- **Affiliate performance** (bringing in new users)

Each category has its own ranking scale, so you can track your performance in each area separately.

To view your ranking in each category:\
Go to **PPL → Top**, then select the category you’d like to explore.

Your Mapper ranking reflects your **expected earnings for the next withdrawal cycle**.\
The withdrawal limit updates continuously based on your activity in the three areas above.

Mappers who consistently:

- Create high-quality content
- Stake early on valuable memories
- Grow genuine communities
- Avoid manipulative behavior

…will rank higher and increase their potential earnings.

For more details on how the withdrawal amount is calculated, please refer to this article - [<u>https://docs.collectivememory.ai/understanding-economy-1-1-withdrawal</u>](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/understanding-economy-1-1-withdrawal)
