## **<u>Why do I see changes in my ranking and my withdrawable amount?</u>**

Mapper ranking is determined by your overall contribution across three key activities:

- **Creating content**
- **Staking in content**
- **Affiliate performance** (bringing in friends and new users)

Your ranking reflects your expected earnings for the upcoming withdrawal date and is directly affected by your expected withdrawable amount.

The withdrawable amount displayed in the app shows exactly what you’ll be able to withdraw at your next withdrawal date. This amount updates continuously based on your activity in the three metrics above, as well as fluctuations in the token’s value.

Mappers who consistently create high-quality content, stake in valuable memories, grow real communities, and avoid manipulative behavior will rank higher and increase their profits.

## **<u>Why does my withdrawable amount keep updating?</u>**

The amount shown is calculated based on your earnings from the last 30 days (a rolling 30-day window).

This means that each day, the system looks back at the previous 30 days and totals those earnings. If you had a particularly strong earning day exactly 30 days ago, once that day falls outside of the 30-day window, it is no longer included in the total. When that happens, the displayed amount may decrease.

This doesn’t mean you’re losing money. It simply means that older earnings have moved outside the current 30-day calculation period.

As long as you withdraw your earnings at least once every 30 days, you will receive everything you’ve earned.

## **<u>Why has my withdrawal amount stayed the same for several days?</u>**

If your withdrawal amount remains exactly the same for a few days, it means that your **base rank currently exceeds your bonus eligibility**.

The withdrawable amount is made up of two main components:

**Base Rank Amount** – A steady, rounded number that does not change often.\
**Total Bonus Amount** – A number that changes daily based on your performance.

Both components measure your performance in the app across three main categories:

- Creating content
- Staking in content
- Affiliate activity

When the **base rank amount is higher than the total bonus amount**, your withdrawable balance will remain fixed.

When the **total bonus amount surpasses the base rank amount**, the difference between the two is added to the base rank. This causes your withdrawable amount to start changing and updating daily.
