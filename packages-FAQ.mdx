## How do Investor Benefits Packages work?

Investor Benefits Packages provide enhanced staking capacity, increased withdrawal limits (for subscription plans), and pATTN rewards. Benefits are activated immediately after purchase or qualifying conversion and remain active according to the package selected.

---

## What is the difference between Subscription and One-Off packages?

### Subscription Packages

Subscription packages provide:

- Increased staking capacity
- Enhanced withdrawal limits
- Monthly pATTN rewards
- Monthly bonus pATTN rewards

Benefits remain active while the subscription is maintained.

### One-Off Packages

One-off packages provide:

- Increased staking capacity
- pATTN rewards equivalent to the package value

These packages are purchased once and do not automatically renew.

---

## Do I need a credit card to access Investor Benefits?

No.

For users who do not have access to a credit card, Investor Benefits can also be activated through a qualifying **ATTN** or **USDC conversion**.

This option was specifically created to ensure all users can access Investor Benefits regardless of their preferred payment method or geographic location.

---

## Can I activate Investor Benefits using ATTN?

Yes.

You can activate Investor Benefits for **30 days** by converting the equivalent package value in **ATTN**.

For example:

| Investor Tier | Equivalent Value |
| :-- | :-- |
| Plus Investor | \$195 worth of ATTN |
| Advanced Investor | \$445 worth of ATTN |
| Pro Investor | \$945 worth of ATTN |

The required ATTN amount is calculated using the live ATTN/USD exchange rate at the time of activation.\
\
Learn how to purchase and convert ATTN to pATTN here - [https://docs.collectivememory.ai/purchase-attn-and-p-attn#purchase-attn-and-pattn](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/purchase-attn-and-p-attn#purchase-attn-and-pattn)

Once the conversion is completed, your selected Investor Benefits tier will be activated for the next 30 days.

---

## Can I activate Investor Benefits using USDC?

Yes.

You can activate Investor Benefits for **30 days** by converting the equivalent value in **USDC**.

For example:

| Investor Tier | USDC Equivalent |
| :-- | :-- |
| Plus Investor | 195 USDC |
| Advanced Investor | 445 USDC |
| Pro Investor | 945 USDC |

Learn how to purchase and convert USDC to ATTN, and ATTN to pATTN here - [https://docs.collectivememory.ai/purchase-attn-and-p-attn#purchase-attn-and-pattn](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/purchase-attn-and-p-attn#purchase-attn-and-pattn)\
\
Once the conversion is completed, the corresponding Investor Benefits tier will be activated for the next 30 days.

---

## How long do benefits remain active when activated through an ATTN or USDC conversion?

Benefits remain active for **30 days** from the date of activation.

At the end of the 30-day period, users may renew their benefits through another qualifying ATTN or USDC conversion or by maintaining an active subscription.

---

## Is there any difference between subscription activation and ATTN/USDC activation?

No.

Users receive the same Investor Benefits regardless of whether they activate them through:

- An active subscription
- A qualifying ATTN conversion
- A qualifying USDC conversion

The benefit tier, staking limits, withdrawal allowances, and reward structure remain the same for the duration of the activation period.

---

## How is the ATTN conversion amount calculated?

The required ATTN amount is determined using the live ATTN/USD market rate at the time of activation.

Because market prices fluctuate, the amount of ATTN required to activate a package may vary over time.

---

## What is pATTN?

pATTN is a platform reward token distributed as part of Investor Benefits packages.

Eligible users receive pATTN rewards based on the package or benefit tier they have activated.

---

## What is Bonus pATTN?

Bonus pATTN is an additional promotional reward provided with subscription-based Investor Benefits tiers.

Bonus pATTN is credited separately from standard rewards and is not redeemable.

---

## How is pATTN converted to ATTN?

The current conversion ratio is:

**4 pATTN = 1 ATTN**

This ratio may be adjusted in accordance with future platform updates.

---

## Are package benefits guaranteed to remain unchanged?

Package offerings, rewards, and benefit structures may evolve over time.

Any changes will be communicated through official platform announcements and documentation updates.

---

## Can I upgrade my Investor Benefits tier?

Yes.

Users may upgrade to a higher Investor Benefits tier at any time. Any additional requirements or activation costs will be displayed during the upgrade process.

---

## Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes.

Subscription-based Investor Benefits can be canceled at any time. Benefits will remain active until the end of the current billing period and will not automatically renew afterward.

---

## What is the standard withdrawal limit?

By default, investors can withdraw up to **10% of their Investor Earnings**.

This limit is designed to support long-term participation while maintaining a healthy balance between liquidity and ecosystem growth.

---

## How do Investor Benefits Packages affect withdrawal limits?

Investor Benefits Packages increase the percentage of Investor Earnings that can be withdrawn.

| Tier | Withdrawal Limit |
| :-- | :-- |
| Standard | 10% |
| Plus Investor | 20% |
| Advanced Investor | 50% |
| Pro Investor | 100% |

The higher your tier, the greater your access to earned rewards.

---

## Why would I want a higher withdrawal limit?

A higher withdrawal limit provides greater flexibility and control over your earnings.

Benefits include:

- Accessing a larger portion of earned rewards
- Increasing portfolio liquidity
- Reinvesting capital more aggressively
- Taking profits when desired
- Managing personal cash flow more effectively

For active investors, higher withdrawal limits can significantly improve capital efficiency.

---

## What is the standard staking capacity?

By default, accounts can stake up to **10,000 units**.

This standard capacity is available to all eligible investors.

---

## How do Investor Benefits Packages affect staking capacity?

Investor Benefits Packages increase the maximum amount that can be staked.

| Tier | Maximum Stake Capacity |
| :-- | :-- |
| Standard | 10,000 |
| Plus Investor | 12,500 |
| Advanced Investor | 15,000 |
| Pro Investor | 20,000 |

Higher tiers allow investors to allocate more capital to staking opportunities.

---

## Why would I want a higher staking capacity?

A larger staking capacity allows you to put more capital to work within the ecosystem.

Potential benefits include:

- Higher earning potential
- Greater participation in staking opportunities
- Improved capital utilization
- Increased exposure to ecosystem growth
- The ability to scale your investment strategy without creating additional accounts

For investors who consistently reach their staking limits, an increased capacity can significantly improve long-term returns.

---

## Do staking capacity increases affect my earnings?

Potentially, yes.

A higher staking capacity allows you to stake more capital. While earnings are never guaranteed, the ability to deploy additional capital may increase your potential earning opportunities compared to remaining at the standard staking limit.

---

## Do withdrawal limit increases change how rewards are earned?

No.

Withdrawal limits only affect how much of your Investor Earnings can be withdrawn.

They do not change:

- How rewards are calculated
- How staking rewards are earned
- How ATTN Savings work
- How vesting works

---

## Do Investor Benefits Packages change the 50% Liquid / 50% Savings distribution?

No.

The **50% Liquid / 50% Savings model applies to all users and all Investor Benefits tiers**, including:

- Standard accounts
- Plus Investor
- Advanced Investor
- Pro Investor

Regardless of package level:

- 50% of earned ATTN is distributed immediately as liquid ATTN
- 50% of earned ATTN is deposited into ATTN Savings

Investor Benefits Packages do **not** alter this distribution.

---

## If I upgrade to Pro Investor, do I receive 100% of my earnings immediately?

No.

The Pro Investor package increases your **withdrawal allowance** but does not change the ATTN Savings allocation model.

All ATTN earnings continue to follow:

- 50% Liquid ATTN
- 50% ATTN Savings

The difference is that Pro Investor users may withdraw up to **100% of eligible Investor Earnings**, while still participating in the same ATTN Savings framework as every other participant.

---

## Do ATTN Savings rules apply to investors, founders, and users equally?

Yes.

The ATTN Savings release schedule is designed as a shared alignment mechanism across the ecosystem.

The same principles apply to:

- Users
- Investors
- Founders

This ensures all long-term stakeholders are aligned around the growth, liquidity, and long-term success of the ATTN network.

---

## Can I combine multiple Investor Benefits packages?

No.

Only one Investor Benefits package can be active on an account at any given time.

Investor Benefits packages do not stack and activation periods cannot be combined.

---

## What happens if I activate a new package while another package is already active?

The most recently activated package becomes your active Investor Benefits tier.

Any previously active package is immediately replaced.

For example:

- You currently have **Plus Investor** active.
- You activate **Advanced Investor**.
- Advanced Investor becomes active immediately.
- Plus Investor benefits are discontinued and replaced by Advanced Investor benefits.

---

## What happens if I activate the same package again?

Activating a package that is already active will replace the current activation with a new 30-day activation period.

Packages do not stack and activation periods are not accumulated.

---

## Can I upgrade my Investor Benefits tier?

Yes.

You can activate a higher-tier package at any time.

The newly activated package immediately becomes your active Investor Benefits tier and replaces any previously active package.

---

## Can I downgrade my Investor Benefits tier?

Yes.

You may activate a lower-tier package at any time.

The newly activated package becomes active immediately and replaces the previously active package.

---

## Does my latest activation always take priority?

Yes.

The most recent qualifying activation—whether through subscription, ATTN conversion, or USDC conversion—determines your active Investor Benefits tier.

Only the latest activated package is considered active.
