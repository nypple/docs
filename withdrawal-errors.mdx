---
title: "Withdrawal Errors"
---

If you encounter an error while attempting to withdraw, you can contact our support team at [**support@collectivememory.ai**](mailto:support@collectivememory.ai).

However, some messages do not require reporting:

1. **<u>Today’s ATTN pool has been fully used</u>** 

A certain amount of ATTN is allocated daily to ensure ongoing withdrawal availability.

In rare cases, there may be a short gap between ATTN allocation batches, which can temporarily trigger this message.

**What to do:**\
 Simply wait 1–2 hours and try again.

2. **<u>The withdrawal is delayed by 2 days</u>**

If your withdrawal request is delayed by 2 days instead of processed immediately, we recommend:

1. **Try to withdraw again in the next availability** .
2. **Increase your contribution across all aspects of the app**.

To be a strong contributor on the platform, you are encouraged to stay active in the **three main areas**:

- **Content Creation** – Creating diverse and inspiring content on a consistent basis.
- **Staking** – Staking in exciting and relevant new content.
- **Affiliate** – Bringing new users to the app.

Users who consistently create high-quality content, contribute across all platform activities, build strong communities, and avoid manipulative behavior should not experience these difficulties and are also expected to trend upward in profits.

If this issue persists, or if you encounter any other error, please contact the support team.
