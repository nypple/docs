## Understanding the withdrawal calculation: 

### **Key Basics**

Before diving into the details, there are two important things to know:

- **Conversion ratio:** pATTN → ATTN is now **4:1** (4 pATTN = 1 ATTN). This is relevant here because withdrawing ATTN is the process of converting pATTN to ATTN
- **Update timing:**
  - Your **pATTN Balance** updates shortly after redeeming profits (creator or investor)
  - Your **Next Withdraw Limit** updates **once every 24 hours**
  - Next Withdrawal Limit uses a rolling 30-day window, if you earn less today than you did 30 days ago, this number will decrease. If you earn more (or less than 30 days since joining) the number will increase

### **Creator Profits**

Creators earn:

- **1% of staked pATTN**
- **10% of injected pATTN**

**Example:**\
If your memory has:

- 500 pATTN staked → you earn 5 pATTN
- 50 pATTN injected → you earn 5 pATTN

**Total: 10 pATTN**

This amount:

- Goes to your **pATTN balance immediately after redeeming**
- Is reflected in your **withdrawable amount after the next daily update**
- Next Withdrawal Limit uses a rolling 30-day window, if you earn less today than you did 30 days ago, this number will decrease

**In short:**\
**Creator profit = 1% (stake) \+ 10% (injections)**

### **Conversion Reminder**

All withdrawal calculations are eventually converted:

- pATTN → ATTN (**4:1**)
- ATTN → USDC (market rate - updating daily)

### **Affiliate Profits**

- You earn **30% of your affiliate’s creator earnings (daily)**
- Earnings go to your **pATTN balance**
- **Reflected in your withdrawable amount every 24 hours** - added to the rolling 30-day window withdrawal calculation

**Example:**

- The users you brought earned 100 pATTN as creators → You get 30pATTN as an affiliate.

### **Investor Profits**

When redeeming a stake, two things matter:

1. **Your profit or loss - Loss positions affect the withdrawal and your balance in the same way that a profit position does**
2. **The off-ramp fee**
