---
title: "Protecting Your Personal Information and Funds"
---

**Never share personal or sensitive information regarding your Collective Memory account or wallet with any individual**—especially your wallet’s recovery phrase.

An official member of the Collective Memory team will never ask for this type of information.

If you want to verify that you are communicating with a legitimate Collective Memory team member, please contact support and ask the individual to continue the conversation via the official support email. 

Please see how to do that in this article - https://docs.collectivememory.ai/contacting-support

**Please make sure to always act carefully and responsibly when managing your funds.**
