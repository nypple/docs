## **My Profile**

You can access your profile by tapping the small bubble with your profile picture and nickname in the upper-right corner of the app.

Inside your profile, you’ll find the following options:

- **Change profile picture** → Tap your profile image
- **Change nickname** → Edit the _User Name_ below your profile picture
- **Log out** → Tap the _Logout_ button above your profile picture
- **Support** → Access helpful information about the app via the _Support_ button
- **Delete account** → Use the _Delete Account_ button below your profile picture
- **Wallet address** → Available in the upper-left corner

### **Account Overview**

Your profile also displays key account metrics:

- Ranking
- pATTN balance
- Current withdrawable amount
- Total ATTN withdrawn
- USDC balance (if you’ve swapped ATTN to USDC)

**Tip:** Some metrics are displayed in both pATTN and projected USD. You can tap on the values to switch between these views.

The withdrawable amount is also shown in both pATTN and USD. Please note that the conversion ratio from pATTN to ATTN is 4:1. For more details, refer to this article:\
[https://docs.collectivememory.ai/understanding-economy-1-1-withdrawal](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/understanding-economy-1-1-withdrawal)

### **Withdraw ATTN**

To withdraw your monthly available amount (convert pATTN to ATTN), simply tap the **“Withdraw ATTN”** button in your profile.

### **Get 20 pATTN**

You can collect **20 pATTN every 24 hours** directly into your balance.

## **Staking**

You can stake on any content within the app.

To learn more, read the full guide:\
[<u>https://docs.collectivememory.ai/guides/staking-attn</u>](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/guides/staking-attn)

**How to stake:**

1. Browse content using the _New_, _Top_, or _Core_ feeds
2. Enter a memory
3. Choose your stake amount using the slider or by entering a value

## **Redeem Investor Profits**

To manage your investments:

1. Go to **My \> Invested**
2. View all content you’ve staked on
3. Sort by profit, latest, highest stake, and more
4. Tap a memory to redeem your stake

## **Collect Creator Revenue**

To collect your earnings as a creator:

1. Go to **My \> Created**
2. View all content you’ve created
3. Tap **“Collect Creator’s Revenue”** at the bottom of the screen

## **Swap & Send ATTN**

- **Swap ATTN (e.g., to USDC):**\
  Go to **My \> Wallet \> Swap**
- **Send funds to another wallet:**\
  Go to **My \> Wallet \> Transfer \> Send**

For more details:\
[<u>https://docs.collectivememory.ai/attn-withdrawal</u>](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/attn-withdrawal)
