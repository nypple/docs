## **<u>Should I use my affiliate code to bring new users?</u>**

Yes! Collective Memory thrives on building strong communities. One of the best ways to contribute to the ecosystem—and increase your potential earnings—is by bringing great new users to the app using your affiliate code.

You can find your unique affiliate code in the app:\
**App → PPL → Viral or Affiliate**

## **<u>What do I receive when I use my affiliate code?</u>**

If a new user joins the app using your affiliate code, you receive **two types of bonuses**:

### **1. Sign-Up Bonus**

Both you and the new user receive a **sign-up bonus**. The reward amount depends on your position in the **Creators ranking (You can check your position in the app \> PPL \> Top \> Creators)**:

- **Top 20 Creator** – 5000 pATTN reward, **no daily limit**
- **Top 100 Creator** – 2500 pATTN reward, **up to 50 users per day**
- **Top 200 Creator** – 1000 pATTN reward, **up to 10 users per day**
- **Top 500 Creator** – 100 pATTN reward, **up to 5 users per day**
- **Above Top 500** **Creator** – 10 pATTN reward, **up to 1 user per day**

Once you reach the **daily limit**, you can still invite new users to your affiliate circle, but **no additional sign-up rewards will be granted for that day**.

### **2. Affiliate Earning Bonus**

For a period of **6 months**, you will receive **30% of the pATTN earnings generated by users who joined through your affiliate code**.

This bonus is credited to **your account balance**, and **not directly to your withdrawable amount**.
