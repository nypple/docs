## **<u>Should I use my affiliate code to bring new users?</u>**

Yes! Collective Memory thrives on building strong communities. One of the best ways to contribute to the ecosystem—and increase your potential earnings—is by bringing great new users to the app using your affiliate code.

You can find your unique affiliate code in the app:\
**App → PPL → Viral or Affiliate**

## **<u>What do I receive when I use my affiliate code?</u>**

If a new user joins the app using your affiliate code, you receive **two types of bonuses**:

### **1. Sign-Up Bonus**

Both you and the new user receive a **sign-up bonus**. The reward amount depends on your position in the **Promoters ranking (You can check your position in the app \> PPL \> Top)**:

- **Top 20 Promoter** – 5000 ATTN reward, **no daily limit**
- **Top 100 Promoter** – 2500 ATTN reward, **up to 50 users per day**
- **Top 200 Promoter** – 1000 ATTN reward, **up to 10 users per day**
- **Top 500 Promoter** – 100 ATTN reward, **up to 5 users per day**
- **Above Top 500** – 10 ATTN reward, **up to 1 user per day**

Once you reach the **daily limit**, you can still invite new users to your affiliate circle, but **no additional sign-up rewards will be granted for that day**.

### **2. Affiliate Earning Bonus**

For a period of **6 months**, you will receive **10% of the ATTN earnings generated by users who joined through your affiliate code**.

This bonus is credited to **your account balance**, and **not directly to your withdrawable amount**.

## **<u>Affiliate Withdrawal Bonus</u>**

The portion that eventually becomes **withdrawable** is calculated as follows:

You receive **20% of the Affiliate Earning Bonus** generated from the ATTN earned by the users you referred.

This **20% applies only to the Affiliate Earning Bonus**, and **does not include the initial Sign-Up Bonus**.

For more details about withdrawal bonuses, please refer to the relevant [<u>article in the help center</u>](https://docs.collectivememory.ai/guides/bonus).
