**<u>How do I know if my account is blocked?</u>**

If you are unable to perform any of the following actions, it is possible that your account has been temporarily blocked:

- Upload content
- Stake
- Redeem earnings (staking profit or creator profit)

If this happens, please contact our support team at [**support@collectivememory.ai**](mailto:support@collectivememory.ai) and include the following details:

- Your **full name** and **country**
- Your **account ID** or **wallet address**
- A **screenshot of the error** as it appears on your end

Our team will review your account and assist you.

**<u>What are the possible reasons for account blocking?</u>**

If your account has been blocked recently, it may be due to one or more of the following reasons:

- Manufacturing non-genuine content
- Excessive spamming activity
- Excessive cross-staking between multiple accounts
- Harassing other user/s within the app

If any of the reasons above are unclear, or if you believe your account was blocked, please contact our support team at [**support@collectivememory.ai**](mailto:support@collectivememory.ai).
