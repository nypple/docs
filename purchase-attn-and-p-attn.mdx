## **<u>How to Purchase ATTN and pATTN</u>**

1. **Transfer USDC to Your Collective Memory Wallet**\
   Send USDC on the **Base network** to your Collective Memory wallet.\
   We recommend sending a **small test amount (2–5 USD)** the first time.\
   \
   **Important:** The transfer **must be made on the Base network**.\
   \
   You can find your Collective Memory wallet address in the app:\
   **Profile → Wallet Address**
2. **Swap USDC to ATTN**\
   In the app, go to:\
   Wallet → Swap → Swap USDC to ATTN\
   Then:
   - Enter the amount you want to swap
   - Confirm the transaction
3. Once the swap is complete, you will have **ATTN** in your wallet. You can either **hold it or use it within the app**.
4. **Convert ATTN to pATTN for Staking in the App**\
   If you want to **stake ATTN**, you first need to convert it to **pATTN**.\
   Go to:\
   Wallet → Swap → Swap ATTN to pATTN\
   Then:
   - Enter the amount you want to swap
   - Confirm the transaction
5. Once your **pATTN balance increases**, you can **use it for staking within the app**.
